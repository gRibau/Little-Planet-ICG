# Project Proposal: Mini Planet

**Course:** Introduction to Computer Graphics (2025/2026)

**Student:** Gonçalo Ribau - 119560

### 1. Scenario
My project proposal consists in a mini earth like planet, with 3D terrain and "traces of civilization". The planet would be viewed from space. 

### 2. Modeling and Animation
The application will demonstrate the modeling and animation of various objects, including the main planet and it's contents:
* **Objects:** Planet with 3d terrain, cities, houses, a cargo ship, a plane and a satelite.
* **Animations:** Automatic movements like the planet's rotation, satelite translation around the earth, plane flying around the earth, ship sailing.

### 3. Illumination and Textures
To enhance the visual composition of the project, I will add many lighting entities and materials to it's contents:
* **Illumination:** Stars and the sun in the background, the latter being an obvious light source and lights in the cities and houses that, when in the sun's shadow, will turn on.
* **Textures and Materials:** Not really sure if i should do something more realistic or cartoonish, so I don't really know if the materials used will be too complex, but they obviously must react to lighting.

### 4. Interactivity
The interactions I'll put in the project are the following:
* **Mouse:** Changing the user's perspective with a fixed position in the planet, rotating the planet around and clicking in various elements of the composition which will lead to animations or more interactivity.
* **Keyboard:** Controling the ship and/or the plane.

*Note: The things mentioned in this proposal are the most basic ideas for the project, some things might be changed or added later.*
